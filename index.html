Hi! I’m Vennila, I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor's degrees in both Statistics and Data Science at the University of California, Berkeley. My academic journey and professional experiences have been driven by a deep interest in harnessing the power of data to influence and enhance government policies, non-profit initiatives, and social impact projects. I’m currently a Data Science Intern at the Berkeley Center for Health Technology where I help analyze and report findings from medical claims data. I’m also a member of Berkeley Data Science Society’s Social Good Committee where I have worked on multiple data consulting projects for non-profit organizations. As I continue on this path, my goal is to deepen my understanding of how data can be vital to creating positive change in government, policy-making, and non-profit sectors. I am keen on exploring opportunities that allow me to contribute to meaningful projects where data is not just numbers but a tool for empowerment and transformation. For more information on my research interests and project experience, you can refer to the Projects page.
